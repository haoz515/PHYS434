= e(1+2)(1s) Exponential of Sum

This is the probability generating function for a discrete random variable with a Poisson distribution: Poisson(1+2) Therefore: X+YPoisson(1+2) Source: wikepedia(https://proofwiki.org/wiki/Sum_of_Independent_Poisson_Random_Variables_is_Poisson)
